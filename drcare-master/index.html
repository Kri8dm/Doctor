Clinic Departments

Explore our diverse range of specialized services tailored to meet your healthcare needs. From diagnostic imaging to laboratory analysis and specialized treatments, our clinic offers a holistic approach to your well-being.

CT Scan Department

Our CT Scan Department provides advanced imaging services for precise diagnosis and treatment planning. Led by experienced professionals, we prioritize patient comfort and safety.

Advanced Imaging

Our CT scans provide detailed views for evaluating tumors, injuries, and vascular issues.

Guided Procedures

We offer minimally invasive procedures, such as biopsies, using CT imaging for accuracy.

Emergency Imaging

Rapid CT imaging helps assess acute conditions for prompt intervention.

Specialized Protocols

Tailored CT protocols include cardiac angiography and virtual colonoscopy.

USG Department

In our USG Departments, we provide comprehensive ultrasonography services for accurate diagnosis and monitoring. Our team of skilled professionals ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the imaging process.

Ultrasonography

Our advanced ultrasound technology allows for detailed imaging of various organs and tissues to detect abnormalities.

Prenatal Imaging

We offer specialized ultrasound scans for monitoring fetal development and assessing maternal health during pregnancy.

Guided Procedures

Ultrasonography is utilized to guide minimally invasive procedures such as biopsies and aspirations with precision.

Doppler Studies

Our Doppler ultrasound services enable evaluation of blood flow, aiding in vascular diagnosis and circulation monitoring.

Mammography Department

In our Mammography Departments, we provide comprehensive breast imaging services for early detection and diagnosis. Our team of experienced professionals ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the screening process.

Mammography

Our advanced technology enables detailed imaging of breast tissue to detect abnormalities at an early stage.

Screening Mammograms

We offer routine mammograms for early detection of breast cancer in asymptomatic women, promoting proactive healthcare.

Diagnostic Mammograms

Used for further evaluation of suspicious findings, facilitating timely diagnosis and treatment planning.

3D Mammography

Our advanced technology provides enhanced imaging for improved detection of breast abnormalities, offering greater accuracy.

Specialized Testing

Rapid Results
