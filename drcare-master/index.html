Clinic Departments

Explore our diverse range of specialized services tailored to meet your healthcare needs. From diagnostic imaging to laboratory analysis and specialized treatments, our clinic offers a holistic approach to your well-being.

CT Scan USG Mammography OPG Pathology X-ray

CT Scan Department

Our CT Scan Department provides advanced imaging services for precise diagnosis and treatment planning. Led by experienced professionals, we prioritize patient comfort and safety.

Advanced Imaging

Our CT scans provide detailed views for evaluating tumors, injuries, and vascular issues.

Guided Procedures

We offer minimally invasive procedures, such as biopsies, using CT imaging for accuracy.

Emergency Imaging

Rapid CT imaging helps assess acute conditions for prompt intervention.

Specialized Protocols

Tailored CT protocols include cardiac angiography and virtual colonoscopy.

USG Department

In our USG Departments, we provide comprehensive ultrasonography services for accurate diagnosis and monitoring. Our team of skilled professionals ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the imaging process.

Ultrasonography

Our advanced ultrasound technology allows for detailed imaging of various organs and tissues to detect abnormalities.

Prenatal Imaging

We offer specialized ultrasound scans for monitoring fetal development and assessing maternal health during pregnancy.

Guided Procedures

Ultrasonography is utilized to guide minimally invasive procedures such as biopsies and aspirations with precision.

Doppler Studies

Our Doppler ultrasound services enable evaluation of blood flow, aiding in vascular diagnosis and circulation monitoring.

Mammography Department

In our Mammography Departments, we provide comprehensive breast imaging services for early detection and diagnosis. Our team of experienced professionals ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the screening process.

Mammography

Our advanced technology enables detailed imaging of breast tissue to detect abnormalities at an early stage.

Screening Mammograms

We offer routine mammograms for early detection of breast cancer in asymptomatic women, promoting proactive healthcare.

Diagnostic Mammograms

Used for further evaluation of suspicious findings, facilitating timely diagnosis and treatment planning.

3D Mammography

Our advanced technology provides enhanced imaging for improved detection of breast abnormalities, offering greater accuracy.

OPG Department

In our OPG Departments, we provide comprehensive dental imaging services for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. Our team ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the imaging process.

Orthopantomography

Our advanced technology enables detailed imaging of dental structures for diagnosis.

Routine Dental X-rays

We offer standard dental X-rays for routine check-ups and monitoring of oral health.

Guided Procedures

OPG imaging guides dental procedures such as extractions and implants with precision.

Specialized Views

Our OPG services offer views for evaluating dental conditions and treatment planning..

Pathology Department

In our Pathology Departments, we provide comprehensive diagnostic services for accurate disease detection and management. Our team ensures precise analysis and interpretation of specimens to aid in patient care.

Diagnostic Testing

Our laboratory conducts a wide range of tests to identify diseases and abnormalities in bodily fluids and tissues.

Biopsy Analysis

We offer expert evaluation of tissue samples obtained through biopsies, aiding in the diagnosis of various medical conditions.

Specialized Testing

Our pathology services include disease-specific tests, ensuring thorough evaluation and treatment planning.

Rapid Results

We prioritize timely reporting of test results, facilitating prompt diagnosis and treatment decisions for optimal patient care.

X-ray Department

In our X-ray Departments, we offer comprehensive imaging services for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning. Our team ensures patient comfort and safety throughout the imaging process.

Diagnostic Imaging

Utilizing advanced X-ray technology, we provide detailed scans to detect abnormalities and injuries.

Routine X-rays

We offer standard X-ray examinations for routine screenings and monitoring of various medical conditions.

Specialized Views

Our X-ray services include techniques tailored for specific medical needs, ensuring thorough evaluation and treatment planning.

Emergency Imaging

With rapid X-ray capabilities, we provide timely assessments of acute medical conditions, enabling prompt intervention and care.
