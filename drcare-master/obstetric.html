NT/NB Scan (12 to 14 weeks)

Raaya Diagnostic Center specializes in NT/NB (Nuchal Translucency/Nasal Bone) scans, conducted between 12 to 14 weeks of pregnancy. This crucial scan assesses fetal development, including the measurement of nuchal translucency and the presence of nasal bone, providing valuable information about the risk of chromosomal abnormalities such as Down syndrome.

With our state-of-the-art ultrasound technology and skilled technicians, Raaya Diagnostic Center ensures accurate and detailed evaluations during the NT/NB scan. Our compassionate team provides support and guidance to expectant parents throughout the process, prioritizing their comfort and well-being. Contact us to schedule your appointment or to learn more about our obstetric sonography services.
